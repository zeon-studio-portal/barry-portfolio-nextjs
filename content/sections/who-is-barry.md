​[Barry Stamos](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bstamos/) has founded, built, and scaled billion-dollar companies and now leads Supernova, a network of 250+ exited founders backing what comes next.\<br />
After 25 years across Silicon Valley startups, Fortune 500 innovation, and venture capital, he believes most organizations are focused on the wrong AI conversation entirely.\<br />
The real risk isn't that AI becomes more powerful. It's that humans fail to evolve alongside it.\<br />
Barry's work explores how AI is reshaping leadership, identity, trust, culture, and decision-making and what it means to stay valuable in a world where execution is a commodity.\<br />
A 3x exited founder, he helped scale Responsys to a unicorn IPO and a \$1.6B exit to Oracle. He speaks from both sides of this disruption: the technology and the humans running it.\<br />
That combination lets him speak to the strategic and deeply personal dimensions of this moment in a way most audiences have never experienced from a stage.
